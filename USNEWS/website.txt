Applying
When applying to Princeton University, it's important to note the application deadline is January 01, and the early action deadline is November 01. The application fee at Princeton University is $65. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due January 01. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 7 percent and an early acceptance rate of 19.9 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
